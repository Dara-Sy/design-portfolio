Client Work



About

Circle Y Saddles is America’s Leading Saddle Brand and started hand crafting saddles in 1960 in the small Texas town of Yoakum.


Problem

Circle Y has good presence across its social media channels, with a almost 33K Facebook Fans. Despite the significant number of followers and good content, posts have a poor reach. That is because social media channels limit the number of followers that see a post. Organic posts do not convert to sales.



Solution

Instagram Ads & Shoppable Instagram, along with Facebook Ads, could turn fans into shoppers by showing posts and products to the best targets. We found potential for growth in our preliminary audit. Soon after that we optimized their data feed so that their products are easier to find, and carried out product level analysis to figure out which of the products were generating traffic, losing traffic, and making conversions.

Facebook Ads

Designs are done in different styles to conduct A/B user tests to determine which ad users respond to better. Conversion Rates are tracked and determined by Facebook Analytics technology.

Facebook Carousel Ads

Facebook Carousel Ads tell a story to customers through images and video in a single ad unit.

The first version carousel tells a more emotional story on the bond between horses and riders.

The second version carousel focuses more on Circle Y products.



Shoppable Instagram Carousel Ads

Shoppable Instagram offer customers to buy through the app, while telling a story to customers through images and video in a single ad unit.


Results

Social campaigns also allow to reach people who have already interacted with the business, such as social media followers who show interest in Circle Y. By optimizing the product feed and analyzing sales at the product level, Facebook & Instagram Ads both increase revenue.
