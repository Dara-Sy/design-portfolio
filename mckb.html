Case Study



About

Manhattan Center for Kitchen and Bath is one of New Yorks's premiere kitchen & bath showrooms, which sells high-end design center for kitchen & baths selling appliances, millwork, flooring, fixtures & tile.


Problem

Manhattan Center for Kitchen and Bath has only 194 Organic SEO Clicks per month, while compoetitors like New York Kitchen and Bath generate about 1.4K SEO Organic Clicks.



Solution

LinkedIn Ads target organizations based on size, location, industry and target specific people (i.e. Job Title, Role, Seniority). LinkedIn Ads test creative, messaging, day of week, call-to-actions, etc.

LinkedIn Ads

Designs are done in different styles to conduct A/B user tests to determine which ad users respond to better. Conversion Rates are tracked and determined by Google Analytics technology.

LinkedIn Carousel Ads

LinkedIn Carousel Ads tell a story to customers through images and video in a single ad unit.

The first version carousel shows one set of kitchen designs.

The second version carousel shows another set of kitchen designs.


Results

Social campaigns also allow to reach people who have already interacted with the business, such as social media followers who show interest in Manhattan Center For Kitchen And Bath. By optimizing the product feed and analyzing sales at the product level, LinkedIn Ads increase revenue.
