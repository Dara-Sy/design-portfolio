Cruelty-Free App


About

Plant-based food in the US went up by 8.1% during the past year, topping $3.1 billion, according to research carried out by Nielsen for the Plant Based Foods Association (PBFA) and the Good Food Institute.


Problem

Vegans look for businesses that support their choices. Sometimes, it's a struggle to narrow down the search for businesses when searching through a pool of diverse choices.


Solution

Cruelty-Free is an app that helps consumers make their search easier for vegan supporting businesses! Searches are filtered by name, location, and business type (ie. cafe). Users can favorite businesses and add reviews.
